How Friday Night Funkin uses cookies to enhance your gaming experience

Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our Friday Night Funkin gaming website. They help us provide you with a better gaming experience by remembering your preferences and improving website performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

At Friday Night Funkin, we use cookies for several important purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website and improve performance
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your gaming experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are vital for the website to work correctly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and cookie consent management.

Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website, which pages are most popular, and identify any technical issues that need fixing.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and preferences, such as your preferred language, region, or game settings, to provide a personalized experience.

4. Specific Cookies We Use

Below is a detailed list of the cookies we use on our website:

  • fnf_session: Maintains your session while playing the game (Session duration, Essential)
  • fnf_preferences: Stores your game preferences and settings (1 year, Functionality)
  • fnf_analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to improve the website (2 years, Analytics)
  • fnf_performance: Monitors website performance and loading times (1 month, Performance)

5. Gaming-Specific Cookie Usage

As a rhythm-based music game, Friday Night Funkin uses cookies to:

  • Remember your high scores and achievements
  • Save your preferred difficulty settings
  • Store your favorite music tracks and customizations
  • Optimize game performance based on your device

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over how cookies are used on our website:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings
  • Cookie Consent: Use our cookie consent banner to manage preferences
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential cookies while keeping essential ones

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect your gaming experience and some website features may not work properly.

7. Data Protection & Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with data protection regulations:

  • All cookie data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy
  • We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without consent
  • Cookie data is stored securely and used only for the purposes described
  • You can request information about the data we collect through cookies
